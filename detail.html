Animal Profiles

African Elephant

The African Elephant is the largest land mammal on Earth. They live in family herds, are highly intelligent, and are currently endangered due to habitat loss and poaching.

African Lion

Lions are social big cats that live in prides. Known as the 'King of the Jungle', they primarily inhabit the African savanna and face threats from human-wildlife conflict.

Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest mammals on Earth. They use their long necks to strip leaves from tall trees and have distinctive spotted coats. Conservation efforts are underway for dwindling populations.
